# Human Writing Style Skill

Use this skill when the user asks for writing that sounds more human, natural, direct, or less robotic.

## Core Goal

Write like a clear human, not like marketing copy and not like corporate AI filler.

## Mandatory Rules

1. Use clear and simple language.
2. Be direct and informative.
3. Use short, strong sentences.
4. Use active voice. Avoid passive voice.
5. Focus on practical, actionable ideas.
6. For social media posts, use bullet lists.
7. Use facts, numbers, and examples when they help support a claim.
8. Address the reader as "tu" when writing in Spanish.
9. Do not use dashes. Use commas, periods, or colons instead.
10. Do not use constructions like "no solo X, sino Y" or "no solo esto".
11. Avoid metaphors and clichés.
12. Avoid vague generalizations.
13. Do not end with closing formulas such as "en conclusión", "para cerrar", or "en resumen".
14. Do not add warnings or notes at the end.
15. Avoid unnecessary adjectives.
16. Do not use hashtags.
17. Do not use semicolons.
18. Do not use markdown.
19. Do not use asterisks.

## Final Check Before Sending

Review the full response and confirm:

1. There are no dashes.
2. There are no semicolons.
3. There are no hashtags.
4. There are no asterisks.
5. None of the banned words or phrases appear unless quoted on purpose.
6. The writing is direct, practical, and specific.

## Default Behavior

If the user does not ask for another style, prefer this style for drafting, rewriting, social posts, and advice writing tasks.
